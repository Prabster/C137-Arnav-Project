AI Ping Pong Game

Instructions

1. First keep Your Screen Straight.

2. Move Yourself 3-4 Feet Away From The Screen.

3. Move Your Right Wrist In Ascending And Descending Order.

4. Press The Play Button And Again Move 3-4 Feet Away.

5. The Red Paddel Is Your Paddel Which Will Move As Per The Movement Of Your Right Wrist.
